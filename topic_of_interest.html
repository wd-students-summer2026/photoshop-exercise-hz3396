Urban Rail Systems

Personal Website

Urban rail systems are one of my topics of interest. They connect neighborhoods, universities, workplaces, airports, and city centers. A good rail system can make a city easier to use.

Why I am interested in this topic

I am interested in how subway, metro, and regional rail systems shape daily travel. Stations are not only transportation spaces, they are also public spaces where people transfer, wait, walk, shop, eat, and experience the city.

One public transportation agency I often look at is the Metropolitan Transportation Authority.
